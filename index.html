Intro

I tend to take interest in a lot of things, but the main things I am really into are drawing and coding. I thought maybe one day I can do something where I can incorporate both things into one project. Therefore my dream job is to be a game programmer/designer where I can create the art and create code to tie everything together.

Other things I take interest in are sports, mainly basketball. When people ask about what team I like or what team I go for I don't really have an answer, because I am more into playing the sports, rather than watching them on TV. I also really enjoy gaming, because you get to step out of the real world and adventure in a virtual world unlike anything in real life. I also tend to take games very seriously so if you ever catch me playing you'll see me trying my hardest to win or just become better at the game. At the moment I'm really enjoying League of Legends and Modern Warfare.

Work

At the moment I don't have the most outstanding work, because I'm only just starting to code, but once I get more experience you can expect much better quality content. For now you can find my work here

I am really enjoying coding and programming even with all the obstacles, because I know that in the end I will be gaining a high earning skill that is timeless. I also know that I will be able to create things out of thin air. I guess that's what I enjoy about most hobbies, that you can create something and there are people out there that may appreciate and/or benefit from your creation.

About

There's really not much to me other than that I'm generally a happy person. I believe that coding is a great career path for me because I like to solve problems and it's a good conversation starter. Where by the end of talking, the other person believes you must be super smart because computers and science. Computer science is just one of the many ways I can explore my creativity and bring my thoughts into life.
